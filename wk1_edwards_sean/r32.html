Nissan skyline r32

The r32 Skyline released in may 1989. It was available as either a 2-door coupe or 4-door hardtop sedan, all other bodystyles were dropped. The r32 featured several versions of the RB-series straight-6 engines, HICAS four-wheel steering, with the rear wheels being hydraulically linked to the front steering. The r32 became one of the first Japanese production cars to feature a 5-speed automatic transmission. Nissan also produced 100 Australian models of the r32.
